BluePrint scale marker

Print this page, cut out the marker, and photograph it next to your part. BluePrint reads it to measure real millimetres from your photo.

This square must measure exactly 50 mm on each side.

This bar must measure exactly 100 mm — print at 100% / Actual size (no "fit to page").

  1. Print at 100% scale, then check the bar above is 100 mm with a ruler.
  2. Cut out the marker square.
  3. Lay it flat beside the part, in the same plane as the face you photograph.
  4. Shoot square-on (as perpendicular as possible) so the marker isn't skewed.
